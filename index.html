Crystal Collector

Game Instructions

  • You will be given a random Target Score at the start of the game.
  • Click on a crystal below to add a specific amount of points to your score.
  • You win the game by matching your score to the Target Score.
  • You lose the game if your score exceeds the Target Score.
  • The value of each crystal will remain hidden from you, and will change when starting a new game.
